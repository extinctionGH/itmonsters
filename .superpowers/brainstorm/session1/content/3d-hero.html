You want to mix the clean Pentagram header with the giant emblem, BUT add high-end 3D interactability. Here is how that works.

The 3D Scrollytelling Interactive Canvas

The Mix: We keep the clean, minimal Pentagram header at the top. But the rest of the screen is a live 3D WebGL canvas (using Three.js / React Three Fiber).

  • Initial Load: A massive, interactive 3D model of the ITMonsters triangular eye floating in the center. It reacts to mouse movement.
  • The Scroll (Jesko Jets style): As you scroll down, the 3D model doesn't just scroll up—it animates. It shrinks, rotates, and locks into the top corner, OR the rigid Pentagram grid slides up from the bottom of the screen, covering the 3D canvas seamlessly.
  • The Vibe: Extremely high-end, blending corporate structural perfection (the grid) with cutting-edge tech (the 3D interactive logo glowing in ITMonsters Pink).
